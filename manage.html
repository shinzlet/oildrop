Manage Data

Export your data

By exporting your data, you can save your Oildrop scripts and settings to a file on your computer. This can later be imported to restore your configuration, or to share your scripts.

Import

Here, you can import data that was previously exported from Oildrop. "Overwrite" will replace your current configuration with the data file - erasing your scripts and settings in the process. "Load Scripts" will leave your existing data in place, and install scripts from the data file alongside your existing ones.
